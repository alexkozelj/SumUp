        How To Play

        GOAL is to beat the computer by takeing as much VALUE CARDS from the table !

        Value cards are all the cards in range from 10 to 14 and each of them caries 1 POINT except:
        10 DIAMONDS - 2 POINTS
        2 CLUBS - 1 POINT

        • 2 ♣
        • 10 ♦

        Sum up combination of cards from the table with ONE of your cards in hand.

        It doesn't matter if it's hearts or clubs, as long as the numbers on cards add up!


        A (ace) = 11 or 1
        J ( jack ) = 12
        Q ( queen ) = 13
        K ( king ) = 14


        NOTICE: ACE (A on the card) can be 11 or 1 !

        POSSIBLE COMBINATIONS

        There are many possibilities to take as many cards from the table



        1. SAME CARDS

        • Q ♦
        • +
        • Q ♥
        • =
        • Q ♠

        • 2 ♠

        • 3 ♠

        • A ♠

        3. PAIR OF COMBINATIONS THAT ADDS UP TO SAME NUMBER

        • 10 ♥

        • +

        • J ♥



        IMPORTANT !!!

        The order of selecting cards is very important!

        Try to select cards in order that adds up to a number that you want to take !

        The ACE in your hand will be calculated as 11 !
        Example: So if you want to take 10 and ACE as 1 for 11, you should select 10 first and then the ACE to add up to 11.
